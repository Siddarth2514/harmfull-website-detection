⚠️ Harmful Websites & Their Risks

Learn about dangerous websites and how to stay safe online.

🚨 List of Harmful Websites

  • Phishing Sites: Fake banking, PayPal logins, stealing passwords.
  • Malware Sites: Free software, movies, games that install viruses.
  • Scam Stores: Fake shopping websites that steal money.
  • Pirated Software: Distributes keyloggers and trojans.

🛑 Causes & Risks

These websites can lead to identity theft, financial fraud, or even legal trouble.

✅ How to Stay Safe?

Always verify URLs, use antivirus protection, and avoid clicking unknown links.
